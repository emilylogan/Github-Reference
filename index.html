Project 1: GitHub Reference

Step 1

Open up and login to Github.

Once logged in, click on the plus sign on the upper right hand corner of the screen.

Click on the plus sign and select, "New Repository".

Step 2

Once you have opened, "Create a new repository" page it is time to name your repository.

Under, "Repository name*" type, "yourusername.github.io".

Select, "Public", and make sure that the, "Initialize this repository with README" is checked.

Step 3

This is the screen you should see

Next, towards the top of the screen select the, "Settings" tab.

You should now see this screen

Step 4

On the, "Setting" page under, "Options" scroll down to, "Githib pages".

Under, "Github Pages", under, "Source" click the down arrow next to, "None" and select, "master branch".

Step 5

After selcting, "master branch" Github will send you to the top of the page.

Scroll back down to, "GitHub Pages" and at the top you will see, "Your site is ready to be published at, "http:yourusername.github.io/yourusername.github.io/"

Click on the blue link and it should send you to your new live GitHub Site!

Congratulations you have successfully created your first GitHub live site!
